FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PharmDigit apps and PharmCalC.

What is PharmCalC and who is it for?
PharmCalC is a pharmacy calculation app designed for PharmD students, pharmacy residents, and licensed pharmacists. It provides 70+ built-in calculators and 2,000+ NAPLEX-style practice problems covering every major calculation domain tested in pharmacy school and on board exams — including dosage calculations, IV drip rates, pharmacokinetics, compounding, pediatric dosing, and chemotherapy calculations.
Does PharmCalC help with NAPLEX preparation?
Yes. PharmCalC is built around NAPLEX-style pharmacy math problems. The app's Quiz and Exams modes include timed practice sessions across 18 calculation categories, with difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard) and dedicated Final Exam modes. Students receive scores, correction percentages, and performance rankings to track board exam readiness.
What pharmacy calculations does PharmCalC include?
PharmCalC covers 18 pharmacy calculation domains: Dosage Calculations, Basic Pharmacokinetics (PK), Pediatric Calculations, Apothecary & Avoirdupois, Clinical Pharmacy, Community Pharmacy, Concentration & Dilution, Density & Specific Gravity, Electrolyte Calculations, Isotonicity & Buffer Systems, Medical Cost Calculations, Parenteral & IV Calculations, Radiopharmaceutical, Reducing & Enlarging, Special Patient Populations, Unit Activity & Potency, Veterinary Calculations, and Chemotherapy Calculations.
Is PharmCalC available on Android and iPhone?
Yes. PharmCalC is available on both iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play). Search for "PharmCalC: Rx Math" on the App Store or "PharmCalC – Pharmacy Math" on Google Play.
What is Pill Manager (Pharm Pills)?
Pill Manager (Pharm Pills) is a medication management app for patients and caregivers. It helps you track medication schedules, set dose reminders, log refill history, record side effects, monitor medication costs, and store insurance information — all stored locally on your device for maximum privacy.
Is my health data safe with PharmDigit apps?
All PharmDigit apps use a local-first privacy model. Your data is stored only on your device — we do not collect, sell, or share personal health data. No user accounts are required, no cross-app tracking, and no advertising identifiers are used.

Pharmaceutical and biomedical research is often slowed by fragmented data, manual analysis, and disconnected systems. Students lack practical, accessible tools. Professionals waste hours on calculations that should take seconds.
